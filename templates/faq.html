I'm a customer. How can I create an NFT?
In order to create an NFT, you need an artist account. Unfortunately, you have to re-register as an artist, using a different email. If you want to delete your old account and transfer your NFTs and cryptocurrencies, please contact us using the Contact form.
I sold an NFT but I didn't receive the whole amount. Why?
As ArtChain, our only revenue is a little fee on every transaction made through our website. For cryptos, the fee is the 2% of the ACH equivalent amount, while for NFTs the fee is 5% of the selling price.
I put an NFT on sale but I changed my mind. How can I keep my NFT?
If you are the owner of the NFT, you can find a button on the item page that allows you to cancel the NFT from the market. If you later change your mind, you can always re-put it on the market, choosing a new price.
I forgot my password. How can I get it back?
If you forgot your password, there is a link in the login page that allow you to get your profile back. Unfortunately, you can't get back your previous password for security reasons, but a new password is sent to you via email. Once you get back your profile, we advise you to change again your password.
I tried to upload an NFT but the system says that the image must be squared. Why?
To allow the maximum quality for all the pages of the website, we require artists to upload only squared images. If your artwork is not squared, please try to resize it.
